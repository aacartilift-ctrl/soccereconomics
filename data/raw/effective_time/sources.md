# Sources for Effective Playing Time Data

Accessed: 2026-03-19

## Primary Sources

1. **ESPN/Opta - "Matches are longer, and there's more late goals"**
   - URL: https://www.espn.com/soccer/story/_/id/38861516/premier-league-biggest-clubs-enjoy-flood-more-late-goals
   - Data: Pre-VAR, VAR era, 2020-23 avg, 2023-24 for all big 5 leagues
   - Contains both ball-in-play AND stoppage time tables
   - Also has late goals / stoppage-time goals analysis

2. **Premier League Official - "How new added time rules transformed the Premier League"**
   - URL: https://www.premierleague.com/en/news/3860720
   - Data: 2020-21 through 2023-24 (partial season), official PL numbers
   - Includes avg match time, ball in play, goals/game

3. **Premier League Official - "Action time in the Premier League on the up"**
   - URL: https://www.premierleague.com/en/news/2991132
   - Data: 2021-22 vs 2022-23 comparison

4. **Opta Analyst - "Ball in Play: Are We Seeing Less Football?"**
   - URL: https://theanalyst.com/articles/premier-league-ball-in-play-are-we-seeing-less-football-2025-26
   - Data: 2025-26 early season (55:00 after 70 matches), historical PL context

5. **Opta Analyst - "Matches Are Longer, But How Much More Football Are We Getting?"**
   - URL: https://theanalyst.com/articles/match-time-ball-in-play-referees-stats
   - Data: 2022-23 vs 2023-24 comparisons

## CIES Football Observatory Sources

6. **CIES Weekly Post 272 (2019)**
   - URL: https://football-observatory.com/IMG/sites/b5wp/2019/wp272/en/
   - Data: 35 European leagues, 2018-19 season, effective time as % of match time
   - InStat data

7. **CIES Weekly Post 242**
   - URL: https://football-observatory.com/Effective-playing-time-in-37-European-2150
   - Data: 37 European competitions, effective time as %

8. **CIES Weekly Post 368 (2021-22)**
   - URL: https://football-observatory.com/IMG/sites/b5wp/2021/wp368/en/
   - Data: 36 leagues, range 54:55 to 63:21, avg just above 59 min

9. **CIES Monthly Report 64 - Pan European match fluidity**
   - URL: https://football-observatory.com/IMG/sites/mr/mr64/en/
   - Data: 37 competitions since Jul 2019, avg 61.3% effective time
   - Key finding: no correlation between effective time % and total match length

10. **CIES Twitter/X - May 2025 Top 10 UEFA leagues**
    - URL: https://x.com/CIES_Football/status/1924399882808267121
    - Data: 2024-25 season, impect data, Eredivisie 56:42, Ligue 1 56:40, Bundesliga 55:05
